About Knowvia

Knowvia is a non-profit organization dedicated to bridging the skills gap in underprivileged communities across South Africa. We focus on providing accessible, affordable, and high-quality education and training opportunities that empower individuals to develop practical skills for employment or entrepreneurship. By collaborating with professionals, volunteers, and organizations, we strive to foster self-reliance, economic development, and social change within these communities.

Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide those without opportunity with access to education opportunities and practical skills training, allowing them to overcome economic and social barriers. We strive to provide an environment in which people may learn, grow, and realize their full potential through a range of courses and seminars. By working with local businesses, volunteers, and educators, we ensure that our programs are not only relevant, but also in accordance with current industry demands. Our ultimate goal is to help people achieve self-sufficiency and make a meaningful contribution to their communities by developing a culture of lifelong learning and support.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be South Africa's leading community-based hub for skill development and education. We intend to have a long-term impact by providing people with the skills and confidence they need to pursue meaningful careers or start their own enterprises. Through our projects, we envision a future in which everyone, regardless of background, has the opportunity to reach their full potential and contribute to the growth and prosperity of their community. We aspire to start a chain reaction of positive change in which empowered people help others, resulting in a vibrant and resilient society.
