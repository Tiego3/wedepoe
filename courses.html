Courses and Workshops

We offer a range of courses and workshops to help individuals in underprivileged communities acquire practical skills that can lead to employment or entrepreneurship. Our programs are designed to be accessible, and tailored to meet the needs of our participants.

Coding

Basic coding fundamentals through real-life scenarios.

Digital Marketing

Understand the fundamentals of digital marketing, including social media, email marketing, SEO, and content creation.

Tailoring and Sewing

Learn the basics of sewing, tailoring, and garment construction, from threading a needle to creating your own clothing.

Carpentry

Hands-on experience in carpentry, from understanding basic tools and materials to building furniture and household items.
